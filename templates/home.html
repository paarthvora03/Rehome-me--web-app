About Rehome me !

Our mission is to bring about the day when all dogs can enjoy a happy life, free from the threat of unnecessary destruction, and help dog owners to rehome a dog and do as much as possible to save a life.

Why Rehome a Dog ?

The number one reason for rehoming a dog instead of buying from a breeder is you will save a dog’s life. Animal euthanasia levels in many shelters are on the increase as they just aren’t finding as many people wanting to rehome a dog as they are receiving abandoned dogs. Currently, there are thousands of dogs scheduled for euthanasia – it just doesn’t make sense to go to a breeder and buy a dog when there are so many dogs that need your help!
